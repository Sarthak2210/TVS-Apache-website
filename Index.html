TVS Apache


The TVS Apache is a brand of commuter bikes made by TVS Motors in India. There are 5 new Apache models on offer with price starting from Rs. 95,000 (ex-showroom). The cheapest model under the series is TVS Apache RTR 160 with 159.7cc engine generating 15.3 bhp of power, whereas the most expensive model is TVS Apache RR310 with 312.2cc engine generating 33 bhp of power.


TVS Apache RR 310


The Ultimate track weapon born out of 35 years of engineering prowess and dominance of TVS Racing. The Apache RR 310 is our ultimate feat of engineering, with relentless performance and innovations like ride modes and throttle-by-wire.


    Key specs:-
  • 312.2cc
  • MaxPower - 33bhp @ 9,700rpm
  • Mileage - 30 kmpl
  • Kerb Weight - 174 kg

TVS Apache 200 4V


The TVS Apache RTR 200 4V with first-in-segment Ride Modes, adjustable suspension and SmartXConnect heralds the future of the Apache RTR Series. Its Race Tuned Fuel Injection engine (RT-Fi) delivers consistent blistering performance and the three ride modes adapts the bike to any race condition. Segment-first technologies such as the RT Slipper Clutch, TVS SmartXConnect, Dual Channel ABS with RLP control and Adjustable Suspension push the boundaries of innovation, making revolutionary technology the core of this powerful machine.


    Key specs:-
  • 197.75cc
  • Max Power - 20.21bhp @ 8,500rpm
  • Mileage - 37kmpl
  • Kerb Weight - 153kg
